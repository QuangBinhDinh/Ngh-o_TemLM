                   **************************HUONG DAN SU DUNG CHUONG TRINH**************************************
Truoc khi su dung chuong trinh, cho tep lib, file dicitonary.jar va file database sql vao cung 1 thu muc. Sau do chay bang cmd...
Vi chuong trinh nay su dung database de them tu vao tu dien, nen username va pass co the khac nhau. Mo project bang IDE va chinh
sua 2 thuoc tinh private String user, va private String pass trong class DictionaryManagement.

Chuong trinh tu dien co 6 chuc nang chinh chay o ham main runDictionary.java. Cac chuc nang gom dich tu, dich van ban, phat am, 
xuat tu ra file, chinh sua tu dien. Cac chuc nang dich tu, xuat tu la chuc nang nguoi dung co the truy cap. Con voi chuc nang chinh
sua tu dien (thuc chat la cac ham trong DictionaryManagement) can co pass de su dung (pass mac dinh la 88888888). 
Luu y chuc nang dich van ban, phat am su dung api google translate, cho nen can phai co ket noi mang moi co the dung chuc nang nay
